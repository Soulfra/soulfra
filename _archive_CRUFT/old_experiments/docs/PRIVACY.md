# Privacy Policy - Soulfra Newsletter

**Last Updated: December 19, 2025**

## What We Collect

When you subscribe to Soulfra Newsletter, we collect:

- **Email address** - To send you newsletter updates
- **Subscription date** - To track when you joined

That's it. No tracking, no cookies, no analytics.

## How We Use Your Data

We use your email address to:

1. Send you newsletter posts when we publish them
2. Send you a welcome email when you subscribe

We **never**:
- Sell your email to third parties
- Share your email with anyone
- Track your behavior
- Use cookies or analytics
- Send spam or promotional content

## How We Store Your Data

Your email is stored in a local SQLite database (`soulfra.db`) on our server.

The database contains:
- Your email address
- Subscription date
- Confirmation status
- Unsubscribe date (if applicable)

We use industry-standard security practices to protect this data.

## Your Rights

You have the right to:

### Unsubscribe Anytime
Click the "Unsubscribe" link in any email, or visit:
https://soulfra.github.io/unsubscribe?email=your@email.com

### Request Your Data
Email us at hello@soulfra.com to request a copy of your data.

### Delete Your Data
Email us at hello@soulfra.com to request permanent deletion.

### Export Your Data
Since we use SQLite, you already own your data. It's just a database file.

## GDPR Compliance (for EU subscribers)

If you're in the EU, you have additional rights:

- **Right to access** - Request a copy of your data
- **Right to rectification** - Correct inaccurate data
- **Right to erasure** - Request deletion (the "right to be forgotten")
- **Right to data portability** - Get your data in machine-readable format
- **Right to object** - Stop processing your data

Email hello@soulfra.com to exercise any of these rights.

## Email Service Provider

We send emails via Gmail SMTP. This means:
- Google processes your email address when we send newsletters
- Google's privacy policy applies: https://policies.google.com/privacy
- We do not share additional data with Google beyond what's required to send emails

## No Tracking

This website does not use:
- Google Analytics
- Facebook Pixel
- Tracking cookies
- Session cookies
- Third-party scripts

We believe in **privacy by default**.

## Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

If you believe we have collected data from a child, email us at hello@soulfra.com and we will delete it immediately.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy occasionally. Changes will be posted on this page with a new "Last Updated" date.

We will never make changes that reduce your privacy rights without notifying you by email first.

## Data Breaches

In the unlikely event of a data breach, we will:
1. Notify all affected subscribers within 72 hours
2. Explain what data was compromised
3. Describe what we're doing to fix it
