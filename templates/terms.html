4. GDPR Compliance (Your Rights)

Under GDPR Article 15-22, you have the following rights:

4.2 Right to Erasure ("Right to be Forgotten")

You can delete your account and all associated data from your profile page. This will:

  • Delete your account from the database
  • Remove all your blog posts
  • Unpublish content from GitHub Pages

4.4 Right to Object

4.5 Right to Rectification

You can edit or delete your posts anytime. All changes are tracked in Git version control.

7. AI & Content Generation

Cal is our AI writing assistant powered by Ollama (local AI model). When you record a voice memo:
