About Soulfra

Soulfra is a self-documenting platform where conversations become content. Talk to our AI widget, and it transforms your ideas into structured blog posts, research, and interactive experiences.

Core Philosophy

  • Conversational First - Talk naturally, generate content automatically
  • AI-Organized - Neural networks classify and tag posts by brand
  • Exportable Brands - Download entire themes as ZIP packages
  • Self-Hosted - Python, SQLite, Ollama. No external dependencies.
  • Open Source - Everything we build is public and modifiable

Technical Stack

  • Backend: Python 3.x (Flask)
  • Database: SQLite (filesystem-from-database pattern)
  • AI: Ollama (llama2 model, local inference)
  • ML: Custom neural networks + scikit-learn
  • Frontend: Vanilla JavaScript (no frameworks)

Why Soulfra Exists

Most platforms lock you in. Substack takes 10% forever. Medium owns your audience. WordPress is bloated. Static site generators require manual work.

Soulfra inverts this model:

  • Own your content (SQLite database)
  • Own your AI (local Ollama inference)
  • Own your brand (exportable themes)
  • Own your deployment (self-hosted, $0/month)
