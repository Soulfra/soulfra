Frequently Asked Questions

How is this different from Yelp or Google Reviews?
Traditional review sites focus on customer opinions ("great service!"). We focus on educational authority. You teach through voice recordings, explaining WHY problems happen and how to prevent them. This builds trust, ranks better on Google for problem-solving queries, and generates higher-quality leads.
What is programmatic SEO (pSEO)?
From ONE 10-minute voice tutorial, our AI generates 50+ landing page variations: city-specific pages, problem variations, solution angles. For example, "why AC makes noise Tampa" + "fix noisy AC St. Petersburg" + 48 more. Each targets different keywords, dramatically increasing your Google visibility.
Do I need to write anything?
No. Just record voice on your phone (10 minutes). Our AI handles: transcription, extracting teaching points, generating blog posts, creating landing pages, SEO optimization, and publishing. You focus on your expertise, we handle the content.
How does license verification work?
We integrate with state licensing boards (FL DBPR, EPA certifications, etc.) to verify your professional license. Your profile shows a verified badge with your license number, building trust with potential customers. Free tier gets basic verification, Pro tier gets real-time API integration.
What's the ROI?
Typical results from ONE tutorial: 500 views/month → 25 leads (5% conversion) → 10 customers (40% close rate) → $3,000/month revenue. At $49/month platform cost, that's 61x ROI. Most professionals create 5-10 tutorials, multiplying results.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes. No contracts. Cancel anytime. Your content remains published (good for SEO), but you can't add new tutorials after canceling. You own your content and can export it anytime.
What trades/professions does this work for?
Any licensed professional who solves problems: plumbers, HVAC techs, electricians, roofers, contractors, locksmiths, appliance repair, auto mechanics, landscapers, pest control, pool service, etc. If you have expertise and a license, this works.
