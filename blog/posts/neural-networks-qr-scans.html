Neural Networks: Learning From QR Scans

Soulfra's ML infrastructure learns from everything you do - especially QR code scans.

The Training Loop

  1. Generate QR Code
  2. Brand creates QR code linking to `/brand/`
  3. QR code tracks: brand_id, scan_location, utm params

    1. User Scans
    2. Phone camera scans QR → redirects to brand page
    3. Scan logged in `qr_scans` table
    4. Session cookie tracks user journey

      1. Neural Network Learns
      2. Which brands get most scans?
      3. Which locations convert best?
      4. Which UTM params drive engagement?
      5. Which users share QR codes most?

        1. Recommendations
        2. Suggest brands to promote
        3. Optimize QR placement
        4. Predict successful campaigns

          5. Session Tracking

          Cookies act as "compiler checkpoints" for the ML transformer:

          • Entry point (QR scan, link, search)
          • Pages visited
          • Time spent
          • Actions taken (signup, discussion, game)

          Future: Transformer Training

          Neural networks will:

          • Predict which content you'll like
          • Suggest ideal collaboration partners
          • Match you with compatible brands
          • Optimize your brand's reach

          All from simple QR code scans!
